STATE OF SAUDI SPACE

Exploring funding, accelerators, and commercial pathways for the Kingdom's space economy.

Saudi Arabia's space sector is accelerating under Vision 2030, with over USD 2.1 billion committed in public funding. The national space market is projected to reach USD 31.6 billion by 2035. Institutional enablers like the Saudi Space Agency (SSA) and CST are creating a robust pipeline of grants, tenders, and accelerators for startups and researchers.
